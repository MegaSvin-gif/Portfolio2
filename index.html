Reflection

Highlights

The project I enjoyed most was Godot Pih 2. I liked writing code, creating the story, and building the levels myself. People who tested it said the story was strong.

The Process

I learned that if I distract myself, I stop finishing work. On the final project I stayed focused, which helped me finish and show better work in class.

The Friction

The biggest challenge was export issues in Godot and learning tricky features like cutscenes, portals, and enemies from tutorials.
